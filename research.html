Evolution of Anticipation in S. cerevisiae

I'm studying the evolution of anticipatory behavior using DNA-barcoded S. cerevisiae in the Petrov Lab (in collaboration with Clare Abreu).

Resource Allocation in Fluctuating Environments

Using models and experimental techniques, I am studying how microbes allocate resources in fluctuating conditions.

Counting the Number of Labelled Histories in Galled Trees

In my rotation in the Rosenberg lab we generalized an algorithm to count the labeleld histories (a useful mathematical property of phylogenetic networks) for phylogenetic trees to galled trees, a class of phylogenetic networks.
